Consultancy

Feed and Nutrition Consultancy: Our consultancy service is provided by the professional, experienced, and highly qualified experts in areas of feed, nutrition, farm and health management of animal and fishes.
We not only provide our services to the farmers but also work with industries, farms, and different organizations. By using our education and experiences we provide our services in the fields of feed production, manufacturing, feed hygiene, quality control and nutritional balances if feed ingredients.

Poultry: To meet the global demand for animal protein, poultry meat is expected to lead the demand. So, the interest in poultry and poultry products have grown tremendously in last 20 years and poultry farming is being popular in all over the world. It can be either small-scale or large-scale operation, every business requires science-based farming methods and nutrition driven feed solution. Agro Solution is one of the nonprofit organizations provides advisory services in the areas of poultry feed, nutrition, farm management and disease management.
Aqua: Unlike the poultry and livestock farming, there is not a large amount of accurate information available for the farmers of aqua industry in our country. We provide a wide range of services and innovative solution to the farmers and industries including aquaculture design and developments, social and environmental impact assessments, fish culture & operational management and performance evaluation.
A large proportion of our aqua consultancy is about aqua feed and nutrition. we have expertise in feed formulation, preparation & processing for a varieties of aquaculture species, maintaining feed quality and ingredients ratio. For the private sectors with our combination of practical aquaculture skills and strong economic and social expertise, we help businesses to design more effective and efficient operations and maximize their production.
