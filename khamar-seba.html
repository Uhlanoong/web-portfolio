Khamar Seba

Khamar Sheba: Khamar sheba is the agro-based call center service of Agro Solution. It is the 24hrs one to one interaction service to the root level farmers of poultry, livestock, and aqua sectors and our technical team. The technical team is from individual technical background with higher qualification and training. They continuously provide technological services to the farmers according to their basic needs by inbound and outbound calls.
